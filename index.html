Welcome to The Pavilion,

We’re throwing a one day festival, that we personally promise will be the best day of the summer.

The idea behind throwing this party was to give everyone something different, a way to end the summer completely carefree. It’s a Crankapalooza filled with great food + drinks, games & activities, and music spun by your favourite local DJ’s in a space you’ve never partied in before. Come out with your friends, and their friends, and have fun underneath the Brickworks Pavillion.
